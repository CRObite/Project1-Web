Abdramanov Kuansyh

My name is Kuanysh, and this name translates as happiness. I was born in 2003 in the village of Mugalzhar in Kazakhstan.

I hardly remember how my childhood passed in the countryside. When I was 5 years old, we moved to Aktobe, the nearest city to my village. I was in elementary school at school number 18. Then we lived in the old part of the city. Then they bought a house in another part of the city. From 2015 to 2020, I studied at the new school number 45.

Since that time, my grades began to deteriorate a little, and my parents enrolled me in taekwon-do and English courses. I didn't really like English and to this day I don't really like learning languages. But I liked taekwon-do. Until 2018, I participated in various taekwondo competitions. But despite the courses, my grades still fell. In 2018, my parents divorced, and I began to live with my mother and sisters. I was still doing taekwon-do, but I stopped learning English. I still have a good relationship with my father.

In 2019, I stopped doing taekwondo and started preparing for the UNT to enter a good university. I started taking preparatory courses. There I met interesting people with whom we communicate to this day.

Graduated from high school in 2020. Passed UNT well. Enrolled as a programmer at MUIT University in Almaty.
